David Southgate, I:Avalanche CEO, is a marketing-communications veteran, with more than 10 year's experience in public relations, marketing, and Internet projects. A frequently published writer himself, David has also run the editorial staff for two major Internet sites, techies.com and PuertoRicoWOW.com. Recognizing the tremendous power the Internet and Internet marketing hold for businesses, David formed I:Avalanche in 2001 and incorporated the company in 2005 with small business funding assistance from the Government Development Bank of Puerto Rico.

I:Avalanche has assembled an outstanding team and identified the most value-oriented, yet powerful and reliable ways to reach your desired market. Our clients (among them start-up and mid-sized companies in the health, technology, tourism and staffing arenas) know and trust us — as evidenced by their continued business and referrals.

Among the keys to the success of our marketing-communications projects is our fresh, bottom-line approach to everything we do.
