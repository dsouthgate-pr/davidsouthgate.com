The hottest pages on davidsouthgate.com.

November 25, 2003

More than 1800 different visitors stopped by to view some 7,000 pages on davidsouthgate.com in November, a great start to this very new Web site.   • Those looking for information about staffing and employment strategies
  • Those looking for great Caribbean getaways

Or heck, you may even by that one guy from my high school who followed my link from classmates.com just to see what I've been up to these 20 years since graduation.
