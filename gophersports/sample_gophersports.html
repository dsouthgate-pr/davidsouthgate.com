Gopher Sports

Minnesota Gophers needed a completely redesigned Web site and a content management system that would allow the public relations department to post information about 22 men's and women's athletic teams.

In addition to information about the teams and games, the site included interactive and 360 degreee views of the University of Minnesota Stadiums, as well as parking and ticket information.

In its first six months, the site contained more than 3,000 Web pages.

Southgate's roles:

  • Producer
  • Content Manager
  • Information Architect
  • Photo Direction

Result:

  • Award winner: Voted 1997 "Best Web Site" by the Minnesota Magazine Publishers Association
